Andela is a global talent accelerator that produces world-class developers and connects them with top employers.

Andela's educational arm, The Andela Institute, is one of the most selective academic experiences in the world, with an acceptance rate below 1%. Andela Fellows, some of the brightest young people in Africa, undergo an intensive training experience with cutting-edge education technology designed to give them the skills and experience needed to thrive as full-time, remote developers for companies around the world. Andela is backed by the founders of companies like Facebook, Ebay, and AOL and has offices in North America and Africa.
