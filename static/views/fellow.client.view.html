  • THE ANDELA FELLOWSHIP

    The Andela Fellowship is a new kind of academic experience. You’ll not only learn how to be a rock-star developer, you’ll also have the chance to work with some of the world’s top tech companies. It’s sort of like university, except instead of paying tuition, you’re earning an income. The initial months of an Andela Fellowship involve immersion training in full-stack development skills. You’ll train at an Andela campus and when you have 1,000 hours of coding experience, you’ll start working as a remote developer for tech companies. During the Fellowship, you’ll work full-time while receiving ongoing professional development to ensure you are equipped to meet the evolving needs of the industry. At the end of your commitment, you’ll have the option to stay with Andela or use your skills, experience, portfolio, and references to open up new opportunities for yourself and your family.

  • THE ANDELA ALUMNI NETWORK

    Today’s Andela Fellows will be tomorrow’s entrepreneurs and business leaders developing the technologies and starting the companies that will change our world. Upon becoming an Andela Fellow, you’ll gain access to this elite global network of developers and leaders who can help you advance your career.
